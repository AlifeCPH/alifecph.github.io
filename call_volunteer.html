Call for Student Volunteers

Are you passionate about artificial life, eager to gain hands-on experience in event management, and interested in networking with leading researchers and practitioners in the field? We invite enthusiastic and dedicated students to join us as volunteers for the upcoming Artificial Life Conference, taking place at ITU from July 22nd to July 26th.

Why volunteer?

As a student volunteer, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Gain valuable experience in event organization and management.
  • Network with renowned experts and fellow enthusiasts in artificial life.
  • Attend conference sessions, workshops, and social events.
  • Contribute to the success of an international conference in your field of interest.

Volunteer responsibilities:

Volunteers will assist with various tasks throughout the conference, including but not limited to:

  • Registration desk support and attendee assistance.
  • Session moderation and technical support.
  • Poster session coordination and participant guidance.
  • Social media coverage and online engagement.
  • Participant assistance and information dissemination.
  • Photography and videography.
  • In total, about 10h for the week.

How to apply:

If you're passionate about artificial life and eager to contribute to the success of the conference, we want to hear from you! To apply, please send us an email to artificiallife2024@gmail.com by July 17. Applicants will be notified of their acceptance by July 19.

Important dates:

  • Volunteer Application Deadline: June 25, 2024 July 17, 2024
  • Notification of Acceptance: June 30, 2024 July 19, 2024
  • Conference Dates: July 22nd - July 26th, 2024

Contact information:

For inquiries and more information about volunteering at the Artificial Life Conference, please contact artificiallife2024@gmail.com.

Join us in shaping the future of artificial life research and innovation. We look forward to welcoming you as part of our volunteer team!

Sincerely, ALIFE 2024 organizational committee.
