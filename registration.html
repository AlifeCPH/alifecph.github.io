The registration for the conference includes all the workshops, tutorials, main track article presentations (oral and posters), boat tour, gala dinner, and opening and farewell receptions. During the registration, there is an option to buy an extra gala dinner ticket, which also includes the boat tour, in case that there is another person attending the dinner and boat tour (e.g, a partner).

For visa purposes, after registering, please write to artificiallife2024@gmail.com, with subject "ALife visa form [YOUR_LAST_NAME]". We will send you back an invitation letter. Please feel free to contact us if you have any special requests concerning visa.
