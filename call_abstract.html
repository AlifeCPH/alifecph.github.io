ALIFE 2024 invites submissions of late breaking abstracts (LBAs) summarizing ongoing research and recent studies in the synthesis and simulation of living systems. The list of topics is the same as the one for regular papers and extended abstracts. LBAs are limited to two pages (references excluded) and will undergo a light review process by the conference organizers. LBAs do not need to be anonymized. The authors of the accepted LBAs will be given the opportunity to present their work in a short talk (10 minutes) or as a poster (to be decided when preparing the conference program). Accepted LBAs will not be included in the proceedings.

Submission process

Submissions should be prepared following the author guidelines (same as the extended abstracts) uploaded using this online form.

The deadline to submit your LBA is on the 8th of July, 2024. This deadline is "Anywhere on Earth" (AoE).

If you have any questions, please contact us at artificiallife2024@gmail.com.
