The 2024 Conference on Artificial Life

Copenhagen, Denmark | July 22-26, 2024

The conference

The International Conference for Artificial Life (ALife) will be held at IT University of Copenhagen, July 22-26, 2024.

The theme of this year is Exploring new frontiers: Building on the ancient traditions of the Vikings to travel to new and strange lands and discover new cultures and traditions, we want to dedicate ALIFE 2024 to the weird and wacky of ALIFE. In particular in a time where academic research is under pressure to be more applied, we will instead dedicate ALIFE 2024 to celebrate the fundamental science of artificial life.
