Mind Matching Event

Join our Mind Matching Event to connect with fellow attendees who share your research interests and goals. This event is designed to facilitate meaningful interactions and foster collaborations among participants. By taking part, you'll have the opportunity to engage with fellow researchers, get to know the community and find academic soulmates ❤️

How to sign up?
You can indicate that you would like to join in the conference sign-up form. We will ask some information about your academic preferences, and algorithmically match you to the optimal group.
